    • Adulting

    It seems as though every twenty-something year old complains about adulthood and how they still don’t have their life together, myself included. My goal was to attempt to solve this problem in an unfamiliar medium (JavaScript) through a sort of how-to. Thus, I designed and coded a website that every day, introduces a new “adulting” tip, trick, or tutorial, linking to an external source for the viewer to read, watch, and learn. Essentially, if you refer back to this website everyday for a year—heck, even make it your browser’s homepage—you’ll undoubtedly find yourself struggling less and less when faced with the typical tasks of adulthood.
