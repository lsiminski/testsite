    • 2015 Calendar

    A passion for hand-lettering and a developing interest in experimenting with unconventional mediums lead me to design this 53-page, photography-based weekly 2015 calendar. After a couple attempts of lettering with frosting, I discovered that—with good script hand-writing—it was a piece of cake (pun intended). With a hairdryer, I melted “1 second a day melts my frosting away” and photographed the reaction every 7 seconds, giving the phrase a literal meaning. The rest of the calendar features dates that move/expand throughout to mimic the frosting and burned holes under every date to add an additional elegant, relatable element.
