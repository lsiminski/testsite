    As a collaboration with Faridah Adam, we designed and constructed a kaleidoscope lamp in order to experiment with the Laser Cutter, CNC Machine, and 3D Printer. There was also one catch: everything printed was to be made up of the same basic shapeâ€”a triangle. After collaborative brainstorming and a long list of crazy ideas, I found a rotating light bulb online to take apart and customize. We used each of the machines to our advantage to create the lamp structure and designed 18 patterns to add to the inside of the outer globe for the light to shine through and create the kaleidoscope effect we were looking for.
